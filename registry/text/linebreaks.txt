Preformatter for people who are lazy to type &lt;p&gt;s, &lt;br /&gt;s, 
and &lt;/p&gt;.

Let's face it, HTML is hard, and us non-html slaves do not need 
treatment that HTML markups do to us.
</p>

This preformat plugin will help you with all the silly linebreaks 
markup.
</p>
