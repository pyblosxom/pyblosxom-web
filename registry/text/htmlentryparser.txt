htmlentryparser
#author Joseph Reagle
#infourl http://reagle.org/joseph/blog/technology/python/pyblosxom-htmlentryparser
#download http://reagle.org/joseph/2003/09/htmlentryparser.py
This file will use a simple HTML file as the entry format. 
The &lt;h1/&gt; is used as the title of the entry; all content after the 
closing &lt;/h1&gt; and the closing &lt;/body&gt; is the body of the entry.
