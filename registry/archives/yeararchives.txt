wbgarchives
#infourl http://bluesock.org/~willg/dev/pyblosxom/
#download http://bluesock.org/~willg/cgi-bin/devtrac.cgi/wiki/wbgarchives
#author Will Guaraldi
Generates links by year for head and foot templates.  When you click on 
those year links, it generates a summary page of all the entries for that 
year.  Read the docstring at the top of the file for installation and more 
information.
