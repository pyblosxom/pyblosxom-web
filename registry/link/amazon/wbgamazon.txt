wbgamazon
#author Will Guaraldi
#infourl http://www.bluesock.org/~willg/dev/pyblosxom/
#download None
Builds Amazon links (with images) from a line in your entry
that looks like:

AMAZON::B00008KA60::The Confluence

Most things are hard-coded in the plugin code.  It could use some 
work, but it's useable.
