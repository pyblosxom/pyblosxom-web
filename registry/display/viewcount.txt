viewcount
#author Will Guaraldi
#infourl http://www.bluesock.org/~willg/dev/pyblosxom/
#download http://bluesock.org/~willg/cgi-bin/devtrac.cgi/browser/trunk/pyblosxom/viewcount.py?format=txt
Keeps track of the number of times a given entry has been
viewed. It populates the $viewcount variable with this
number for display. It adds one to the viewcount every time
the entry is viewed.  It stores the information in a
pickled dict in your content directory (or wherever you
want).
