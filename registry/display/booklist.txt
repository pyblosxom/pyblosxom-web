<p>
  Reads from a <tt>::</tt> delimited file a series of books and how far 
  you are through reading them.  It displays the 5 least done books 
  in the <tt>$(booklist)</tt> variable.  If you go to blog/booklist, it 
  shows the complete list of books.
</p>
