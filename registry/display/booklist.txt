book list
#author Will Guaraldi
#infourl http://bluesock.org/~willg/cgi-bin/devtrac.cgi/wiki/booklist
#download http://bluesock.org/~willg/cgi-bin/devtrac.cgi/wiki/booklist
Reads from a :: delimited file a series of books and how far 
you are through reading them. It displays the 5 least done books 
in the $booklist variable. If you go to blog/booklist, it shows 
the complete list of books.
