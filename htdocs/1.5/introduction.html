Introduction¶

This is the manual for Pyblosxom.

This manual will always be free. It’s licensed under the MIT License along with the rest of Pyblosxom. The manual is version controlled along with the rest of Pyblosxom in the Git repository at https://github.com/willkg/pyblosxom/ .

This manual is a work in progress. In many cases, missing information is denoted with a FIXME in the text.

You can help us fix problems by sending in patches. You can find us on the mailing lists or IRC. Details on contact information are in the Project details, contact information, and where to go for help.
